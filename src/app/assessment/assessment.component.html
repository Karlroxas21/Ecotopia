Trivia Game

  1. What is the most abundant greenhouse gas in the atmosphere?

    Carbon dioxide
    Methane
    Nitrous oxide
    Water vapor

  2. What is the main cause of the current global warming trend?

    Natural cycles of the Earth
    Volcanic eruptions
    Human activities that release greenhouse gases
    Changes in the Sun's intensity

  3. What is the term for the measure of how much global temperatures have risen since pre-industrial times?

    Global warming potential
    Global heat index
    Global temperature anomaly
    Global climate index

  4. What is the name of the international agreement that aims to limit global warming to well below 2°C above pre-industrial levels?

    Kyoto Protocol
    Paris Agreement *
    Montreal Protocol
    Copenhagen Accord

  5. What is the main source of energy for most renewable energy technologies?

    Biomass
    Geothermal
    Nuclear
    Solar

  6. What is the process by which plants and other organisms convert light energy into chemical energy that can be used for food and fuel?

    Photosynthesis
    Respiration
    Decomposition
    Fermentation

  7. What is the term for the large-scale movements of water within the oceans that affect climate and weather patterns?

    Ocean currents
    Ocean waves
    Ocean tides
    Ocean eddies

  8. What is the term for the natural phenomenon that occurs when warm water in the eastern Pacific Ocean shifts to the western Pacific Ocean, causing changes in global weather patterns?

    El Niño
    La Niña
    Monsoon
    Typhoon

  9. What is the term for the long-term change in the average weather patterns of a region or the planet as a whole?

    Weather variability
    Climate variability
    Weather change
    Climate change

  10. What is the term for the scientific study of the interactions among organisms and their environment?

    Ecology
    Biology
    Geology
    Meteorology
