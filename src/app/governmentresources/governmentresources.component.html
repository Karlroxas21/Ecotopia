Data and Information

The following resources from government organizations in the United States offer information on potential approaches for addressing climate change.

Climate Data Initiative

Data pertaining to climate change that can provide insights and assist in educating and readying communities, businesses, and individuals in the United States.

U.S Climate Resilience Toolkit

Offers scientific resources, data, and knowledge to support individuals in effectively addressing climate-related risks and opportunities, as well as enhancing their ability to withstand and recover from extreme events.

National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration

This platform offers a range of resources, including advanced computing systems, cutting-edge models, real-time observations, and future projections, to equip individuals with the necessary data, tools, and information for comprehending and planning for climate variability and change.

U.S Department of Energy

This document outlines the approaches presently being undertaken or contemplated to decrease carbon emissions and tackle the issue of global climate change.

Environmental Protection Agency

Resources designed to facilitate learning and comprehension of environmental concerns, along with suggestions for adopting eco-friendly lifestyles.

U.N Framework on Climate Change

The online newsletter of the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change, focusing on matters related to the long-term convention on climate change established by the U.N.
