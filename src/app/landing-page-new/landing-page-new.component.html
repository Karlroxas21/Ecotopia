Eco-Friendly Waste Disposal Practices: A Key Strategy for Climate Change Mitigation

Proper waste disposal is crucial for mitigating climate change as it reduces the release of harmful greenhouse gases into the environment. As an example, not shredding paper and compressing bottles before disposal can make recycling and waste management more efficient, decreasing the carbon footprint associated with waste processing and helping to combat climate change.
