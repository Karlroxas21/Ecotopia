Responding to Climate Change

Mitigation and adaptation are crucial elements in addressing climate change. Mitigation focuses on reducing the causes of climate change, while adaptation aims to manage its impacts. A comprehensive approach that integrates both strategies is vital for establishing a sustainable and resilient future in the context of climate change.
