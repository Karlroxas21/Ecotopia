Problem with the Jeep Outdated Engine

Jeepneys in the Philippines often have outdated diesel engines that lack modern emission control technologies.
As a result, these engines produce higher levels of pollutants, which have adverse effects on both human health, leading to respiratory and cardiovascular problems, and the environment, contributing to climate change through increased greenhouse gas emissions and causing air pollution due to the formation of ground-level ozone and smog.

Causes of Climate Change

Climate change is primarily caused by human activities that release greenhouse gases, such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation. While natural factors also play a role, the current rapid pace of climate change is largely attributed to human activities and their impact on the Earth's atmosphere. environment.

Effects of Climate Change

Climate change has diverse and significant effects on natural systems and human societies, necessitating mitigation efforts to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and adaptation strategies to enhance resilience. International cooperation, policy interventions, technological advancements, and behavioral changes are vital for addressing these effects and creating a sustainable future.
